About Me

Web design refers to the design of websites that are displayed on the internet. It usuallyrefers to the user experience aspects of website development rather than software development.... A web designer works on the appearance, layout, and, in some cases, content of a website.

What web-designers do?

Web design identifies the goals of a website or webpage and promotes accessibility for all potential users. This process in volves organizing content and images across a series of pages, integrating applications and other interactive elements.
